Web Developer / Software Engineer
----------------------------

I've been a serial entrepreneur for over 10 years, learning the ends and out of developing and running small businesses, which has giving me tons of experience. During my entrepreneurial adventures I had the opportunity to utilize my html and css skills by building my own websites and even helping others with theirs. Now I have switched gears to learning how to code professionally and start a career in Software Engineering as I have discovered I actually really enjoy it. Solidifying my skills as a web developer by adding the language Javascript and industry standards to my designs has increased my potential as I hone in on full stack development. Python is a second language that I am acquiring to my knowledge base. As you can probably tell, I am an avid learner. I love to learn no matter how challenging the subject, I am inspired to push through until I feel comfortable with the information. I love solving problems, and I don't feel alive unless I have a purpose. I hope to meet some really cool people in the industry and collaborate on some awesome projects.
